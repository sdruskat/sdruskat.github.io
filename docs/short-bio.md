## Basic bio

> Stephan Druskat is a software engineering researcher at the University Library at Technische Universität Braunschweig, Germany, and a Fellow of the Software Sustainability Institute (UK). He co-founded de-RSE – Society for Research Software. His research interests include research software sustainability, software citation and publication, and empirical and evidence-based (research) software engineering. He is member of the Society for Research Software Engineering, the German Society for Research Software, the Association for Computing Machinery, and the German Association for Computer Science, where he co-founded the special interest group on research software engineering.
